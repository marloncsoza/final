Frequently Asked Questions about GB

What is the weather like in Gordons Bay?

Gordons Bay has some slightly muggy months, with moderately humid months on the other side of the year. The least humid month is April (43.9% relative humidity), and the most humid month is May (62.9%). Wind in Gordons Bay is usually calm.

Where is the best place to fish in Gordon's Bay?

It’s located on Clarence Drive in Gordon’s Bay, and you can stop over at the nearby Spark’s Bay Beach where you can braai, picnic, and fish. There is an entrance fee but you will not regret it. Opposite Sparks Bay Tidal Pool, you will see the entrance for the Klipspringer Trail (Steenbras Nature Reserve).

When is the best time of the year to visit Gordon's bay?

The best times to visit Gordons Bay for ideal weather are August 6th to January 21st based on average temperature and humidity from NOAA (the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration).

What does the GB on the mountain stand for?

Interestingly, these initials don’t stand for Gordon’s Bay, but rather for General Botha, which was the original name of the Naval College named after General Louis Botha who was the first premier of the Union of South Africa
